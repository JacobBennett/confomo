

Features
- Mark a new Friend as met
- Mark an old Friend as to-meet
- Suggest a new Friend to mark as met
- Suggest an old friend to mark as to-meet

Scenarios
- Given that I am at a conference
  When I meet a new friend
  Then that person is marked as met 
- Given that I am at a conference
  When I meet an online friend
  Then that person is marked as met
- Given that I'm preparing to attend a conference
  When I want to meet an online friend
  Then that person is added to my list of friends to meet
